Privacy Policy for Just Use F*cking Kotlin

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Overview

Just Use F*cking Kotlin (JUFK) is a demonstration application built with Kotlin Multiplatform and Compose Multiplatform. This privacy policy explains our approach to user data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.

The Just Use Kotlin app:

  • Does not require user registration or account creation.
  • Does not access your contacts, location, or device storage.
  • Uses PostHog for basic, anonymous analytics (e.g., counting app launches and screen views).
  • Uses a random, anonymous ID to distinguish between different devices without identifying the individual user.
  • Does not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-Party Services

This app uses PostHog for anonymous analytics. PostHog collects basic usage data (e.g., app launches and screen views) using a random, anonymous device identifier. No personally identifiable information is collected. For more information about PostHog's privacy practices, visit posthog.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The app is safe for all ages as we only collect anonymous analytics data that cannot be used to identify individual users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.
